Bugs

Find a bug? Include the bug’s URL, use case scenario (the steps you took to trigger bug), and screenshots in your email. Expect fixes in a minimum of 1 week, depending on complexity.

Content Update

To report content errors (e.g., misspellings, wrong images) or request changes to current content, include the page URL with your content needs. Expect updates within 5 business days.

Email Campaign Form

Request a dedicated email campaign. Remember: submission is not approval. We prioritize email submissions that follow GET/GROW/KEEP principles, are highly targeted, drive revenue or provide added subscriber value. Expect a timeline of 7 business days once final assets are received.

Event or Promotion

Use this form to list upcoming promotions, mention new partnerships or announce key TWC public activities on the Events page. Expect updates within 5 business days.

Global Network List Update

Request new station call letters or modify existing ones. Allow up to 5 business days for this update.

New Project

Complete this form to have your project proposals and enhancements to TWC.com assessed. Keep in mind that we review requests weekly on Thursdays only. Unfortunately, we can’t guarantee that every project will be approved, but we’ll follow up with you if we need more info.

Press Release

Submit your press release and expect it to go live within 1-2 hours during normal business hours. You can also specify the date and time for it to go public.

Programming Legal Notice Update

Ask for programming and broadcast legal changes using this form. Expect updates to post within 1-2 business days.

Sweepstakes Form

Make someone a winner. Use this form to post sweepstakes. Allow a minimum of 1 week for creative depending on complexity and scope of offer.

TWC Store Info Update

Let the world know that a new TWC Store has opened, or that an old one has closed, moved or changed hours. Allow 1-2 business days for new information to post.

Vanity URL

When asking for vanity URLs, be sure to include the underlying URL redirect. Allow up to business 5 days for vanity URLs to properly redirect.

What happens next.

Thanks for your submission. We’ll contact you if we need more information. To check on status, feel free to inquire with the project owners.
